About Orderneario

Orderneario is a location-aware food ordering experience built with:

  • 🌍 Leaflet for maps
  • 💾 Supabase for auth, storage, and real-time sync
  • 💌 EmailJS for dual-sided email flows
It’s modular, scalable, and built with care for both store owners and hungry consumers.
